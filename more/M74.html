Messier 74

Information

Messier 74 is a "grand design" spiral galaxy; it has two prominent, well-defined spiral arms. The gas & dust in spiral arms gets compressed, triggering star formation. This heats the dust, which is what this Herschel image shows. Brighter regions show more star formation. Herschel takes pictures in "slices" of the far-infrared spectrum, which astronomers compare to work out the temperature of the heated dust.

Mass: 100 billion solar masses

Diameter: 87 thousand light years

Constellation: Pisces the fish

Distance: 30 million light years
